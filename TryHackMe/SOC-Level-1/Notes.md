
**What I Learned: Blue Team & SOC Fundamentals**
This section covers the foundational concepts of defensive security and the operational structure of a Security Operations Center (SOC).

SOC Roles & Operations: Broke down the day-to-day responsibilities of a Tier 1 Security Analyst, including incident triage, monitoring workflows, and the general structure and career progression within a blue team.

The Human Attack Vector: Explored why people are consistently targeted through social engineering and phishing, and how a SOC actively monitors and defends against user-targeted attacks.

System Vulnerabilities & Defense: Looked at the defensive side of system exploitation, specifically how threat actors take advantage of misconfigurations and unpatched infrastructure, and the mitigation strategies needed to secure those environments.

Overall, this provided a clear perspective on how organizations actively monitor and defend their networks, offering a strong defensive contrast to standard vulnerability assessments and offensive operations.

**what i Learned: SOC Team Internals**
Completing the SOC Team Internals path gave me a solid, practical understanding of a Level 1 analyst's daily workflow, moving way beyond just staring at raw logs. I learned that my primary job as a first responder is triage—carefully investigating data to separate actual threats (True Positives) from everyday network noise (False Positives). To do this accurately and avoid alert fatigue, I realized how critical it is to use corporate context, relying on asset and identity lookups rather than guessing, and following structured workbooks to ensure no investigation steps are missed. When I do verify a real threat, I now know how to properly document the incident using the "Five Ws" and efficiently escalate it to Tier 2 analysts for response. Finally, understanding core SOC metrics like MTTD, MTTA, and MTTR really tied it all together, showing me how my speed and accuracy directly impact the team's Service Level Agreements and overall security posture.
