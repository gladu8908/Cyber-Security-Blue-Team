## Module 2
- **Title:** Key SOC functions and services across incident response phases (detect, respond, recover)
- **Status:** Completed on 2026-04-28

### Overview
- This module focuses on the key functions and services a SOC provides throughout the incident response phases: detect, respond, and recover.

### Detection, Response, and Recovery
- The SOC detects incidents using monitoring, alerting, and threat intelligence to identify suspicious activity.
- The SOC coordinates incident response activities, including triage, containment, eradication, and recovery.
- Effective incident management requires coordination with other departments such as IT Operations, Legal, and Human Resources to handle technical remediation, legal obligations, and personnel issues.

### Importance of Collaboration
- Collaboration between the SOC and other departments ensures incidents are managed efficiently, organizational impact is minimized, and regulatory/compliance requirements are met.

### Learning Approach and Content
- The module included examples, scenarios, and review questions that explain how SOC teams detect incidents, coordinate responses, and work with stakeholders to recover systems and preserve evidence.
